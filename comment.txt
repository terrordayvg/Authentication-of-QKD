Att_Aut.py - performs one way authentication realistically with Eve haar randomly generating states.
User_Aut.py - performs one way authentication realistically with distance, storage time, lambda defined.
DNN_binary_classification.py - performs binary classification for distinguishing the user and the attacker in the post-processing of the authentication protocol.
